Press "start game/skip" to start.
Press "check answer" to check answer.
Press "yes" or "no" to record your score.
Since passages are randomly selected, sometimes there might be some non-meaningful passages, press "start game/skip" if you want to skip this round (not counting to score) before checking answer.
You can also press "skip" after checking answer to skip the round.
known bug: sometimes buttons disappear. In that case, just click "start game/skip" to resume
